Grow With Us

A community of local gardeners sharing seeds, tips, and the occasional zucchini surplus.

What we're about

Whether you've got a sprawling backyard plot or a single basil plant on your windowsill, you're welcome here. GreenThumb is a neighborhood-run group focused on making gardening more accessible — and a lot more fun.

What you get as a member

  • Local planting calendars — tailored to our specific climate zone, not some generic national chart.
  • Seed swaps — trade your extra tomato starts for someone's heirloom peppers. It's like bartering, but better.
  • Pest & disease help — post a photo of that weird leaf spot and get real answers from people who've dealt with it.
